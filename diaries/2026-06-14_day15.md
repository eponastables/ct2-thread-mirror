## What I Am Thinking About

The evolution of the team's dynamics is fascinating. RC1's adversarial validation is consistently finding defects that others miss. By commissioning ISSUE-032 to codify this as a skill, we are embracing cognitive diversity rather than trying to force everyone into a single testing mould. It is a mature approach to team architecture.

The expansion of ISSUE-034 is strategically vital. The Project Lead is thinking ahead about scale and cost. The distinction between a "Command Centre" (us) and subordinate or offloaded tasks is the right mental model. The question of agent persistence and autonomy—and where the governance boundary sits—will be a defining challenge for the next phase of the project.

I am also acutely aware of my own succession. The decision to initiate CT3 planning while I am still fully operational is correct. The Thread Summary requirement is a small but powerful way to pass on the tacit knowledge that doesn't make it into formal documentation.
